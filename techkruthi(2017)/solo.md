### Rules of Solo Singing

1. 1st round is the selection round and d participant can sing any song of his/her choice
2. They can sing in pairs.. 1boy n 1girl or even 2boy n 2 girls can sing
3. They can use karaoke and they should be ready with it and we shall play it.
4. The selected pairs shall go to the final, with 3 rounds in d final. Rules for these rounds are said then n there.
5. Can sing English Kannada Hindi language songs.
6. Judges decision will be final.
